Experience & Projects

View ExMAG poster

Design of Balloon-Borne Payload Module

Designed a lightweight dust collection payload for micrometeorite sampling in the upper stratosphere using agar-agar gel. Developed a rack-and-pinion tray system with 15:90 gear ratio powered by BLDC motor and delivered a 1.5 kg flight-ready prototype. Presented at Indian Institute of Astrophysics' Modern Engineering Trends in Astronomy 2022 and NASA's ExMAG Fall 2022, selected for testing at TIFR-Hyderabad high-altitude balloon facility.

Thermal analysis of Rectangular Duct

Performed transient thermal simulations in ANSYS to evaluate temperature induced stress concentrations regions. Achieved a factor of safety (FOS) of 1.45 in critical regions and 2.0 in non-critical zones by performing topology optimizations, validating the chamber safe for high pressure combustion testing.

Design & Analysis of Combustion Chamber

Designed a transparent strand burner for UAV propulsion research to withstand 40 bar internal pressure while enabling optical access for laser diagnostics using a borosilicate glass window and aluminum casing.

CFD Analysis of Magneto-rheological Fluids

Conducted comprehensive CFD analysis of magneto-rheological fluids for adaptive prosthetic applications. Analyzed fluid behavior under varying magnetic field strengths and developed computational models to predict performance characteristics for medical device applications.

Structural Analysis of Tiltrotor hub

Performed static FEA analysis of a hinge less tiltrotor hub made of AISI 4340 steel to extract flap, lag and torsional stiffness across radial stations including collar, flex-beam, center & tip. Implemented fixed boundary conditions in ANSYS to generated stiffness profiles and validated against analytical results for improved aeroelastic modeling.

Structural Analysis of CHIRON Wheel RIM

Designed a 2D shell model of a Bugatti Chiron wheel rim in SolidWorks and analyzed a novel aluminum carbon fiber composite showing 15% strength to weight improvement and reduced deformation over conventional aluminum and steel alloy wheel rims.

Design and Analysis of an Industrial Table

Conducted FEA and structural optimization of a 5 ton die support table for an injection molding company increasing load capacity from 2 to 6 tons by refining rubber wood layering and beam design and recommended in house machining to reduce outsourcing and improve quality control.

Square Composite Beam

Designed, developed, fabricated, simulated and tested a 24.5" composite hollow balsa core, ±45° carbon fiber-epoxy wrap beam using vacuum bagging to withstand a 7,200 lbf load, performing 3-point load testing and Tsai-Hill stress analysis. Conducted Tsai-Hill stress analysis, identified buckling and delamination failure modes, and enhanced structural performance by increasing wall thickness and improving resin bonding.

Spacecraft Thermal Design

Designed a six-node thermal network for a spacecraft electronics enclosure using nodal energy balance equations and custom view factor modeling to simulate solar infrared and albedo radiation in both hot and cold orbital cases. Predicted steady-state temperatures between 238°K & 300°K across all subsystems using EES and validated thermal control via passive MLI and Z93-coated radiator without additional hardware ensuring all components remained within operational limits.

3D Printed Bridge Design

Designed and simulated a 3D printed bridge in SolidWorks. Performed FEA to identify stress concentrations, refined the geometry, and experimentally tested prototypes. Ultimately reduced the final design weight from 30.4 g to 12 g while maintaining structural integrity under a 20 lbf load.
